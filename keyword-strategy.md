### 1.2 Intent Categories

The Sādhak content serves three search intents that need distinct keyword treatment:

**Exploratory intent** — "What is [Sanskrit term]"
People looking to understand concepts. Long-tail, lower competition. High potential for educational content that ranks.

**Practice intent** — "How to practice [technique]"
People with enough knowledge to want method. Medium competition, high conversion to retained readers.

**Discourse intent** — "Is [spiritual claim] true / accurate / supported"
Post-rationalist seekers checking claims. Lower search volume but highest quality of audience match.

### 1.3 The Sanskrit Bridge Opportunity

This is the primary keyword differentiation strategy. Almost no content ranks for:
- "[Sanskrit term] vs [Western psychology term]"
- "[Sanskrit term] explained for [modern context]"
- "[Ancient practice] and [neuroscience domain]"

These are the SEO gaps this content ecosystem can own.
